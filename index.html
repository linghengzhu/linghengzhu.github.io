Bio: I joined Waterloo in 2011 and was awarded a Tier-2 Canada Research Chair in 2015. From 2006 to 2011 I was a Senior Member of Research Staff at AT&T Labs. I have a BSc in Computer Science from the University of Toronto (2001; with High Distinction) and a PhD in Computer Science from the University of Waterloo (2006; with Alumni Gold Medal).

Research interests: Big data; Fast data; Dirty data; Data analytics for a sustainable future; Educational data mining. For more information, see my Data Science Lab webpage.

Current projects: job scheduling and data placement in distributed systems; data stream analytics; integrated systems for real-time and historical analytics; discovering column dependencies and business rules from data; graph/social network analytics; analyzing smart electricity/water meter data; analyzing electric bicycle usage data (see WeBike); analyzing co-op employment data. 

Current professional service: Associate Editor for Information Systems, Review Board Member for PVLDB 2017/2018, PC member for SIGMOD 2018

Current teaching: Data Mining (undergrad), Big Data Analytics (grad).  Course notes here
